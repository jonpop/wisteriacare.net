Business Info

Wisteria Senior Care is a 6-Bed home licensed under the rules of Adult Foster Care located in Oakland County, MIchigan. Because of how our program is structured, including staff training and the experience of the administrator, we can serve varied populations. These include those who are aged and who may require help managing incontinence, nutritional and medication routines. In addition we are also qualified to serve those suffering from all stages of Alzheimer's and dementia, the physically handicapped, and those suffering from traumatic brain injuries.
